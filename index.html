The Flight Diary of Love

Jay Chou

Lyrics

At the edge of the equator, skies are clear and blue,

A thousand times I’ve said, “I love you” — each echo felt like new.

Hills by the shore, rugged and steep,

Waves crash loud, but your voice I keep.

I froze our love in Antarctic ice,

A crystal vow that won't melt twice.

My heart won't change, it’s set so true —

This choice I made, to cherish you.

(So clear) so clear, (so strong) so strong,

We rise against where weights belong.

The fading view behind our flight,

Each scene, each shade, now sharp and bright.

I spark my engine, leave dawn behind,

Just speed remains in love’s design.

Nearer the stars, through galaxy’s stream,

I make my wish with steady gleam.

Chorus:

I fly for love, escaping gravity’s hold,

To find a star and name it for you in gold.

Across the light-years, my feelings stay true,

A silent journey — just to reach you.

I fly for love, defying the earthbound chain,

In the weightless void, where there’s no pain.

Through endless space so vast and wide,

I arrive for you, from the other side.
